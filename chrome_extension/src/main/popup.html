Welcome to the Treehouse Subreddit Finder!

Treehouse helps you find new Subreddits by showing you the timeline of submissions for links on Reddit. Look out for the green tables under Reddit links to external sites e.g. Imgur, which will lead you to parts of Reddit, and the Internet who share an appreciation for the linked content.

Additional features which may interest you:
  • We add a button to your Reddit tab bar which lets you toggle Treehouse's visibility.
  • Each link on your page will show you the time, subreddit, and Karma score of each submission in this link's history.
  • You can browse to the Subreddit by clicking on it, or take a look at the comment section for a submission by clicking on the date.

About Treehouse

This extension works by having your browser request submission histories for each link on your page. In order to figure out how to make Treehouse more useful and fun, I collect some anonymous data on usage patterns.
